Medication

Some of the guidelines to be followed during home quarantine are:
● Use oximeter to calculate your blood oxygen saturation level and also keep a monitoring chart of your body oxygen level. In case of falling level immediately reach a nearby hospital.
● A triple layered mask should be used and should be renewed within 8 hours.
● Boost your water intake and plenty of fluids is highly recommended.
● Check your body temperature frequently using a digital thermometer.
● Use an alcohol based sanitizer and wash your hands frequently.
● A proper diet chart should be followed. Adding Vitamin C and Zinc rich food will help in boosting your immunity.
● Consult a doctor for specific medications.

NOTE: Remdesivir should not be taken without precription.
